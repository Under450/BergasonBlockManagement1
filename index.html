Why Choose Us

Bergason Block Management offers a complete professional property management service for freeholders, residential management companies and property developers.

Specialised Expertise: With over 37 years in the property and legal industry, we focus on customer service, ensuring dedicated attention to your property needs.

Direct Contact: We operate a 24hr, 7days a week service for emergencies to ensure issues are dealt with rapidly before they escalate.

Cost Efficiency: Our clients often experience a 15–25 percent reduction in expenditure in the first year, lowering service charges.

Personalised Service: As a family-run business, we prioritise personal communication and swift responses.

At Bergason we listen to our clients’ needs and provide tailored management solutions that meet those needs efficiently.

We deliver flexible services that allow freeholders or directors to maintain appropriate levels of control while benefitting from our expertise.
