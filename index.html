Why Choose Us

Bergason Block Management offers a complete professional property management service for freeholders, residential management companies and property developers.

With over 37 years in the property and legal industry, we focus on customer service, ensuring dedicated attention to your property needs.

We operate a 24hr, 7 day a week service for emergencies. This ensures all issues are dealt with and hopefully abated before further issues arise.

Our clients often experience a 15–25 percent reduction in expenditure within the first year which directly leads to lower service charges.

As a family-run business, we prioritise face-to-face communication and swift responses to address any concerns our clients may have.

We offer tailored solutions allowing freeholders or directors to make discretionary expenditure decisions without heavy administrative burden.

Changing block management companies is not the logistical nightmare many believe. Our transition process typically takes 5 to 7 days.

  • Collecting service charges
  • Information for accountants
  • Service charge arrears recovery
  • Liaising with residents
  • Contractor procurement
  • Routine repairs and inspections
  • Health and safety compliance
  • Annual resident meetings
  • Record-keeping and governance
