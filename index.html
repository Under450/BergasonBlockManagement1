Why Choose Us?

Bergason Block Management provides a complete professional property management service for freeholders, residential management companies and developers.

Specialised Expertise

With over 37 years of combined property and legal experience, we deliver an exceptionally high standard of customer service and operational management.

Direct Contact & Emergency Support

We operate a 24/7 emergency service, ensuring issues are addressed promptly before they escalate.

Cost Efficiency

Most developments experience a 15–25% reduction in expenditure within their first year of switching to Bergason, leading to significantly lower service charges.

Personalised & Family-Run

We prioritise face-to-face communication, rapid response times and a hands-on management approach tailored to your development.

Tailored Block Management Solutions

We have listened carefully to directors and leaseholders, developing flexible management solutions that adapt to the specific needs of each development.

Whether you require a full hands-on management team or prefer to retain involvement with discretionary decisions, we provide the level of control you require.

Switching block management companies is not a difficult process—our typical transition period is 5–7 days, with seamless onboarding and immediate service continuity.

Our Typical Block Management Duties Include:

  • Preparing/distributing service charge budgets & estimates
  • Collecting service charges & handling arrears
  • Financial preparation for independent accountants
  • Providing auditors with required documentation
  • Routine arrears collection (non-litigation)
  • Providing management updates to residents
  • Liaising with residents’ associations
  • Contractor procurement & quotation management
  • Regular site inspections & maintenance oversight
  • Periodic health & safety checks
  • Annual residents’ meetings (if required)
  • Supervision of scheme managers
  • Maintaining resident & tenancy records
  • Providing policy advice to freeholders & boards
  • Working with ombudsmen & advisory bodies

This is not an exhaustive list – it represents the baseline service delivered by Bergason on instruction.
