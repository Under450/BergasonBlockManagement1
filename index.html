Garside v RFYC (2011)

This case clarified “reasonable” service charges under s.19 LTA 1985.

  • Affordability of leaseholders is relevant.
  • Works may need phasing.
  • Urgent works may proceed even if costly.
  • Tribunal can reduce charges if unreasonable.
×

Waaler v LB Hounslow (2017)

Distinguished repairs (mandatory) from improvements (discretionary) and required financial consultation.

  • Leaseholder financial circumstances must be considered.
  • Consultation must be meaningful.
  • Discretionary improvements require heightened scrutiny.
×

Holland v Oxford CC (2002)

Strengthened rights to inspect invoices under Section 22 LTA.

  • Landlords must provide full records.
  • Prevents hidden charges.
×

Francis v Phillips (2014)

Confirmed total cost of works matters, not artificial splitting.

×

Arnold v Britton (2015)

Strict interpretation of lease terms even if outcome is harsh.
