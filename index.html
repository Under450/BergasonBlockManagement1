Why Choose Us?

Bergason Block Management offers a complete professional property management service for freeholders, residential management companies and property developers.

Specialised Expertise: With over 37 years in the property and legal industry, we focus on customer service, ensuring dedicated attention to your property needs.

Direct Contact: We operate a 24hr, 7 days a week service for emergencies. This is to ensure all issues are dealt with and hopefully abated before further issues could arise.

Cost Efficiency: Our clients often experience a 15–25% reduction in expenditure within the first year, leading to lower service charges.

Personalised Service: As a family-run business, we prioritise face-to-face communication and swift responses to address concerns.

At Bergason we’ve listened to our clients’ needs with regards to their block management and have developed property management solutions to accommodate their specific needs — delivering tailored solutions that allow us to manage your developments in the most effective way possible.

We can provide a bespoke service that offers the flexibility you need — freeing you, the freeholder and/or directors, to make discretionary expenditure decisions without the mountains of paperwork you might experience elsewhere.

We can offer a total hands-on approach; a Director will deal with your block personally, or step back to allow the right amount of day-to-day control in your hands if required. This versatility has helped Bergason become a recommended block management company offering a comprehensive service that gives you freedom and peace of mind.

If you’ve been told that changing block management companies is a logistical nightmare — it’s not. We have everything in place to do this for you, with a normal transition period of just 5–7 days. You may need to get used to our rapid response times — we work when you need us, with emergency assistance available 24 hours a day and contractors ready to respond immediately where your property is at risk.

Typical Block Management Tasks Include:

  • Preparing and distributing service charge budgets/estimates.
  • Collecting service charges.
  • Preparation of invoices prior to examination by an independent accountant.
  • Providing information to auditors for annual accounts.
  • Collecting routine service charge arrears.
  • Providing management information to residents.
  • Liaising with residents' associations.
  • Employing contractors and arranging quotes.
  • Inspecting the property and reporting/arranging repairs.
  • Periodic health & safety checks.
  • Holding annual resident meetings if required.
  • Supervision of scheme managers.
  • Maintaining resident and tenancy records.
  • Advising landlords on management policy.
  • Working with ombudsmen and regulatory bodies.

This is not an exhaustive list, but represents the minimum level of service once Bergason have been instructed.

Our fees range from £14–£18 + VAT per property per month, depending on services required.

We recommend a free site visit to understand your needs and tailor a package accordingly.

Additional fees for works outside scope (e.g. Section 20) can be provided once an agreement is reached.
