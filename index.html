Why Choose Us

Bergason Block Management offers a complete professional property management service for freeholders, residential management companies and property developers.

Specialised Expertise: With over 37 years in the property and legal industry, we focus on customer service, ensuring dedicated attention to your property needs.

Direct Contact: We operate a 24-hour, 7-day-a-week service for emergencies to ensure issues are dealt with promptly before escalation.

Cost Efficiency: Our clients often experience a 15–25 % reduction in expenditure within the first year, lowering service charges.

Personalised Service: As a family-run business, we prioritise face-to-face communication and swift responses.

At Bergason we listen to your needs and deliver tailored management solutions that meet those requirements efficiently.

We offer flexible service levels allowing freeholders or directors to choose their level of oversight while benefiting from our expertise.
