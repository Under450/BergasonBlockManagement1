Garside v RFYC (2011)

This landmark case examined “reasonably incurred” costs under s.19 LTA 1985. Affordability and the financial impact on leaseholders were confirmed as relevant considerations.

  • Tribunal may require phasing of works
  • Affordability is a material factor
  • Urgent works may proceed despite high cost
×

Waaler v LB Hounslow (2017)

Distinguished between mandatory repairs and discretionary improvements and required meaningful consultation including financial consideration.

×

Holland v Oxford CC (2002)

Reinforced the right to inspect invoices and supporting documents under Section 22 LTA 1985.

Francis v Phillips (2014)

Prevented landlords artificially dividing works to avoid Section 20 consultation.

Arnold v Britton (2015)

Confirmed strict interpretation of service charge clauses even if financially harsh.
