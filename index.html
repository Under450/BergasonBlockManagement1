Garside v RFYC (2011)

  • “Reasonably incurred” includes financial impact.
  • Phasing works may be required.
  • Urgent repairs override affordability.
×

Waaler v LB Hounslow (2017)

  • Repairs vs improvements distinction.
  • Financial hardship must be considered.
  • Consultation must be meaningful.
×

Holland v Oxford CC (2002)

  • Leaseholders entitled to see underlying invoices.
  • Strengthened rights under LTA 1985.
×

Francis v Phillips (2014)

  • Cannot split works to avoid Section 20.
  • Total cost must be considered.
×

Arnold v Britton (2015)

  • Clear lease wording prevails.
  • Fairness does not override contract text.
