Additional Block Management Services

The services typically included within our block management package include:

  • Preparing and distributing service charge budgets.
  • Collecting service charges.
  • Preparing invoices prior to independent examination.
  • Providing financial information for annual accounts.
  • Routine arrears collection.
  • Liaising with residents and associations.
  • Employing contractors and obtaining quotes.
  • Inspections and handling non-major repairs.
  • Periodic health and safety checks.
  • Annual resident meetings where required.
  • Maintaining owner and tenancy records.
  • Corporate governance support for RMC/RTM companies.
