Garside v RFYC (2011)

  • Reasonableness includes impact on leaseholders.
  • Works may need phasing.
  • Urgent repairs may override cost arguments.
  • Affordability is relevant.
×

Waaler v LB Hounslow (2017)

  • Distinguishes repairs vs improvements.
  • Financial hardship must be considered.
  • Consultation must be meaningful.
×

Holland v Oxford CC (2002)

  • Leaseholders entitled to supporting invoices.
  • Reinforced inspection rights under LTA 1985.
×

Francis v Phillips (2014)

  • Cannot split contracts to avoid Section 20.
  • Works must be viewed as a whole.
×

Arnold v Britton (2015)

  • Courts uphold clear wording even if harsh.
  • Contract interpretation takes precedence.

RTM Support

The RTM Process

  • Assess eligibility (50% participation).
  • Form RTM company.
  • Serve participation notices.
  • Serve claim notice to freeholder.
  • Handover on acquisition date.

What Bergason Does

We act as RTM Company Secretary and Managing Agent, preparing notices and ensuring smooth takeover.

Benefits

  • Control over budgets.
  • Choice of contractors.
  • Direct accountability to residents.
