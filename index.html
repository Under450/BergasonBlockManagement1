Why Choose Us?

Bergason Block Management offers a complete professional property management service for freeholders, residential management companies and property developers. We combine corporate-level compliance and governance expertise with the attention and personal service of a family-run practice.

With over 37 years in the property and legal sectors, we ensure full compliance with legislation, leases, RICS standards and The Property Institute guidance.

We operate a 24-hour, 7-days-a-week emergency service ensuring issues are handled rapidly, preventing escalation. Clients often experience a 15–25 percent reduction in expenditure in the first year due to structured tendering, oversight, and proactive management.

We prioritise face-to-face communication, personal service, and swift responses. Directors may choose full hands-on management or delegate operational authority while retaining discretionary control.

Changing block management companies is not complex — we typically complete transitions within 5 to 7 days.
